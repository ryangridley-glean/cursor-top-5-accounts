Discovery

Questions To Validate & Uncover Blockers

Discovery-oriented prompts for executive conversations — hypotheses to test, not a product pitch.

Strategic priorities

  • Which of revenue, cost, or risk is the binding constraint on your 2026 roadmap right now?
  • Where does engineering velocity most directly move Manhattan Active adoption and cloud conversion?
  • How are you measuring success on agentic AI and Agent Foundry delivery?
  • What would a meaningful improvement in PR throughput or cycle time be worth to the business?

Current state & tooling

  • Walk me through how code moves today — from ticket to production. Where does the most calendar time burn?
  • What AI coding tools are in use today? What's working, and what's still team-level vs. standardized?
  • Where does tribal knowledge hunt slow down onboarding to Active's WMS / TMS / OMS surfaces?
  • Who owns developer-tool standardization, security review, and procurement for AI tooling?

Pilot & success criteria

  • If we scoped a 30-day pilot with one squad, which team and use case would produce the clearest signal?
  • What metrics would convince you — and your security team — that this is worth expanding?
  • What does your ideal 30/60/90-day rollout look like for a new engineering platform?
  • Who else needs to be in the room for a working session on pilot scope and governance?

Assumption check

  • Where does my understanding of Manhattan's priorities match your world — and where is it off?
  • What's the biggest blocker between today's engineering process and hitting your top initiatives?
  • Is GitHub Copilot the incumbent, or are teams running a mix of tools?
  • What would make this a clear yes vs. a "come back in six months"?
